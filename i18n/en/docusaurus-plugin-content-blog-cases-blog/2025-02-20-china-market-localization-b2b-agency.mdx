**Client**: Overseas enterprise, original website had machine-translated content with business logic not matching Chinese user habits

## Challenges

- Content was machine-translated, not localized
- Business logic inconsistent with Chinese user decision-making habits
- Lacked marketing persuasiveness

## Deliverables

- **Business Logic Restructuring**: Information architecture redesign
- **Full Website Copywriting Rewrite**: Based on Chinese market user habits
- **Platform Migration**: Wix Studio to WordPress
- **Responsive Adaptation**: Mobile/tablet/PC optimization across all devices
- **Server Deployment**: Security configuration + SSL
- **Performance Optimization**: China access speed optimization

## Results

Complete website restructuring and platform migration delivered, significant improvement in website performance and user experience

## Tech Stack

WordPress, Astra, Elementor Pro, Linux, Nginx, SSL

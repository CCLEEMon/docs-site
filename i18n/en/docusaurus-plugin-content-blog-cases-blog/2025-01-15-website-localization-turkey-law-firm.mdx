**Client**: Turkish legal and business service provider serving Chinese clients and international enterprises

## Challenges

- Chinese website had no conversion strategy, content focused on feature listings
- B2B enterprise clients and C2C individual clients shared the same information architecture
- Missing high-demand services (bank account opening, Apostille certification)

## Deliverables

- **Audience Segmentation**: Restructured B2B and C2C information logic and copywriting separately
- **Complete rewrite of 6 pages of Chinese content** (Homepage, About, Real Estate, 4 service sub-pages)
- **Integrated authentic endorsement data**: 1000+ cases, 100+ enterprise clients served
- **Wix CMS content implementation**, fixed navigation broken links and page jump issues
- **Proactively identified mobile/tablet/desktop display inconsistencies** and provided fix recommendations
- **Exceeded contract scope by documenting English version structural conversion gaps**

## Results

All Chinese content approved by client team, English version optimization suggestions adopted

## Tech Stack

Wix CMS, Chinese Copywriting, Bilingual Content Strategy, UX Audit

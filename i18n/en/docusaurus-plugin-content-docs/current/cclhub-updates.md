# CCLHUB Changelog

## AI Image/Video Generation

You can now use AI to generate images and videos. Multiple styles and sizes are supported, suitable for creating product display images and marketing materials.

## AI Text Model Upgrade

AI text features are now more powerful, supporting more scenarios. Whether writing product descriptions, responding to customer service messages, or generating marketing copy, everything can be done quickly.

## Cloud Configuration Sync

Your settings are automatically saved to the cloud. If you change computers or reinstall the browser, your configuration will automatically restore after logging in, no need to reset.

## Account Data Migration

After the system upgrade, your account data will automatically migrate to the new structure, no need to worry about data loss.
